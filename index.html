The Sydney Linux Users Group (SLUG) is a community of passionate freedom and technology lovers in Sydney, Australia. We are one of the largest and most active Linux users groups in the world, with a diverse membership of users, developers, sysadmins, and advocates.

Monthly Meetings

SLUG meetings are normally held on the last Friday of every month except December. Current event details can be found on the meetup group page.

SLUG meetings are held at Google Australia. See the following page for details instructions for getting to the meeting.
